Our Mandate

Committed to Namibian Development

The Improvement Network Trust (TINT) is a legally registered body under the Trust Administration Act, 2023, dedicated to societal betterment.

We tirelessly strive to bring about positive change and improvements in education and ethical leadership across sectors in Namibia. This is our core commitment to a brighter future.

Our mission is to effect positive societal change that culminates in improved outcomes for underserved Namibians.
